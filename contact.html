PRIVACY POLICY

Your privacy is very important to us. Accordingly, we have developed this Policy in order for you to understand how we collect, use, communicate and disclose and make use of personal information. The following outlines our Privacy Policy.

Before or at the time of collecting personal information, we will identify the purposes for which information is being collected. Generally when you register we ask for information such as your name, email address, birth date, gender and personal interests.

We will collect and use of personal information solely with the objective of fulfilling those purposes specified by us and for other compatible purposes, unless we obtain the consent of the individual concerned or as required by law. Generally we use such information to customize the content you see, fulfill your requests for products and services, improve our services, contact you and conduct research for internal purposes.

We will only retain personal information as long as necessary for the fulfillment of those purposes.

We will collect personal information by lawful and fair means and, where appropriate, with the knowledge or consent of the individual concerned.

We will protect personal information by reasonable security safeguards against loss or theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use or modification.

We will make readily available to customers information about our policies and practices relating to the management of personal information.

You may modify or delete this personal information or opt-out by clicking on the delete account / Ads in the welcome email you received.  You may also use the contact us link found on the bottom of pages to notify us if you are having difficulty opting out.

Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to your website.

Google's use of the DART cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to your users based on their visit to your sites and/or other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the advertising opt-out page. (Alternatively, you can direct users to opt out of a third-party vendors use of cookies by visiting the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.)

We will make readily available to customers information about our policies and practices relating to the management of personal information.

Because we strive to make our chat rooms family friendly, we keep transcripts of the chat sessions to ensure no one is using our website to lure children and other innocent beings into unwanted materials. Transcripts of all chat sessions are also kept and will be provided upon request from an authorized agency.

We are committed to conducting our business in accordance with these principles in order to ensure that the confidentiality of personal information is protected and maintained.
